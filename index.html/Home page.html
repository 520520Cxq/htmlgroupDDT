Steven Paul Jobs

Stephen Paul Jobs (English: Steven Paul Jobs, February 24, 1955 - October 5, 2011), commonly known as Steve Jobs (English: Steve Jobs), was an American Inventor, entrepreneur[4], marketer, one of the co-founders of Apple, former chairman and CEO, NeXT album maker and founder, Pixar short film producer and former chief executive Executive officer, member of the Board of Directors of The Walt Disney Company in 2006. The place where Apple's launch event will forever be held on September 12, 2017, is named the Steve Jobs Theater after him.
